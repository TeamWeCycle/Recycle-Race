Recycle Race

"The greatest threat to the world is the belief that someone else will save it." -Robert Swan, author
Recycle Race is an app designed to help people of all ages learn how to recycle, created by our team, WeCycle. Our app includes many different functions to educate our users quickly and efficiently in a fun way. Such features include a game, quiz, tips screen, a page which lists different organizations and ways to help your community, and more!
